Mufti Dr Ismail Menk is a leading global Islamic scholar born and raised in Zimbabwe. 
He studied Shariah in Madinah and holds a Doctorate of Social Guidance from Aldersgate University.
Mufti Menk’s work has gained worldwide recognition and he has been named one of
“The Top 500 Most Influential Muslims in the World” since 2010.
He has millions of followers across his social media platforms. 
His personable style and down to earth approach has made him one of the most sought 
after scholars in our time. He has endeared himself to people 
with his much loved lecture series, a Mufti Menk hallmark.
Mufti travels the world spreading a simple but profound message: 
“Do good, help others while preparing for the Hereafter”.
